## Meeting Etiquette Quick Checklist

**DO:**
- ✅ Listen more than talk
- ✅ Ask permission before showing anything

- ✅ Accept "not interested" gracefully
- ✅ Take notes

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Interrupt
- ❌ Use words like "wrong", "error", "inconsistent"
- ❌ Show long technical dumps unsolicited
